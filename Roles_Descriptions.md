**USF Data Science Club -- Proposed Leadership Structure**

**1. President & Strategic Lead**

- Leads strategic direction, oversees all club operations

- Build relationships with faculty, sponsors, and student orgs

- Facilitates team meetings and long-term planning

- Delegate external connections to the relevant department by forwarding
  them to either Finance or Marketing, depending on the nature of the
  partnership or inquiry

**2. Vice Presidents (Executive Level)**

![Shape](/media/image2.png){width="1.0416666666666666e-2in"
height="1.0416666666666666e-2in"}

**✅ VP of Operations & Communication (formerly Secretary)**

**(Leads: Operations & Communications Manager)**  
**Responsibilities:**

- Oversee internal communications and logistics

- Ensure timely email reminders and updates to members

- Track attendance for internal board, take meeting notes

- Create announcements on discord (WhatsApp, Google Drive, Discord
  channels)

- Ensure elections and application processes run smoothly

![Shape](/media/image3.png){width="1.0416666666666666e-2in"
height="1.0416666666666666e-2in"}

**✅ VP of Finance & Funding Strategy (formerly Finance)**

**(Leads: Finance & Budgeting Analyst and Events Coordinator)**  
**Responsibilities:**

- Manage the club's budget (track income/expenses in Excel/Sheets)

- Handle vendor communications (e.g., food orders, space booking)

- Submit receipts for food and event reimbursements

- Apply for Student Government (SG) funding opportunities

- Research grants, sponsorships, and funding from external organizations

- Create financial reports for general board meetings

- Collaborate with VP of Events to estimate event costs

- Maintain digital records of all financial transactions

- Manage the club's bank account (if we decide to have one)

![Shape](/media/imageb.png){width="1.0416666666666666e-2in"
height="1.0416666666666666e-2in"}

**✅ VP of Events & Logistics**

**(Leads: Events Coordinator)**  
**Responsibilities:**

- Plan, schedule, and execute club events (workshops, socials, guest
  talks)

- Coordinate event-day logistics: room setup, cleanup, AV setup

- Prepare RSVP forms, check-in sheets, and manage attendance

- Ensure all supplies (plates, napkins, utensils) are available

- Maintain a checklist template for repeat events

![Shape](/media/imagec.png){width="1.0416666666666666e-2in"
height="1.0416666666666666e-2in"}

**✅ VP of Marketing & Digital Presence (formerly Marketing)**

**(Leads: Marketing & Brand Strategist)**  
**Responsibilities:**

- Manage social media accounts (Instagram, LinkedIn, Discord,
  BullsConnect)

- Create event graphics, story posts, and live coverage

- Write captions and announcements in a consistent tone

- Schedule regular content: member spotlights, recaps, promo posts

- Take event photos and write post-event LinkedIn summaries

- Coordinate with other student orgs for event cross-promotion

- Track engagement (likes, shares, attendance) for reporting social
  media

- Have a dedicated team to communicate with external orgs (Close
  communication with Finance)

**✅ VP of Data Science Workshops & Resources**

**(Leads: Technical Content)**  
**Responsibilities:**

- Create and curate materials for workshops and technical programs, such
  as team projects and hands-on learning activities

- Ensure workshop content is accessible to members

- Test materials prior to workshops to ensure smooth delivery and that
  members can easily follow along

- Maintain a repository of past workshop materials for member reference

- Manage and organize all technical content on the Discord server

- Propose suitable programs that help members improve their skills in
  Data Science

- Coordinate with guest speakers to ensure they have necessary technical
  support

- Gather feedback on workshop content for continuous improvement

- Collaborate with VP of Events & Logistics
